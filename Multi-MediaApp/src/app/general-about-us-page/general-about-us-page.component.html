Haki Martin

Hey, I'm Haki. I have a background in the arts as a sequential artist and illustrator, so I made an image gallery to display images by URL.

I made the scrolling image gallery using JavaScript/TypeScript. The gallery loops on itself to give a nice 'infinite scroll' effect. It is meant to be a sort of showcase (like a super light museum tour), but I also gave the option to edit and delete images from the gallery. You can add to the gallery with the form on the bottom. Items are added and deleted from the gallery dynamically with user input.
