Haki Martin

My name is Haki. I have been illustrating, creating comics and stories, and developing video games my whole life. I love the creative side of the human experience, and so I made a photo gallery. I want people to share their favorite pieces of art, whether it be a movie poster, a photograph, a character design, or anything they want. As they say, a picture is worth a thousand words.

Users will be able to upload an image URL to our database. The app will display all images in the database in a horizontally-scrolling section of the window. If the image was uploaded by that user, they will see an option to delete or update the image. The gallery will scroll infinitely until all images are loaded.
